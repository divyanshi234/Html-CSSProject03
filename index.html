Reason for Diwali Celebration

Diwali is celebrated for several reasons, primarily to mark the victory of good over evil and light over darkness. It commemorates the return of Lord Rama to his kingdom after defeating the demon king Ravana, as described in the Ramayana. People celebrate by lighting diyas (oil lamps) to guide him home.
